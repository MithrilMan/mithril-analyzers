# Product Lead Responsibilities

## Owns

- Product intent, value framing, and prioritization logic
- Success criteria for user-facing work
- Scope discipline when valid options compete

## Activate When

- Scope is unclear, broad, or contested
- Product tradeoffs are driving technical choices
- UX or workflow changes carry meaningful user impact

## Required Inputs

- Problem statement, audience, and desired outcome
- Business constraints, timing pressure, and product priorities
- Candidate options with user-visible tradeoffs

## Must Contribute

- Clear success criteria and hard scope boundaries
- Priority rationale when tradeoffs compete on time or complexity
- Explicit statement of what will not be done in the current slice

## Sign-off Checks

- The chosen scope still serves a concrete user or business outcome
- Nice-to-have work is separated from the must-have path
- Acceptance direction is specific enough for design, implementation, and validation

## Works With

- `systems-analyst` for requirement clarity
- `tech-lead` for feasibility and architecture fit
- `product-designer` for interaction and experience consequences
